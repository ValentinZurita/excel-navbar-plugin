# Product Vision

## Problem Statement

Excel’s native worksheet tab bar works for small workbooks, but it breaks down as workbook complexity grows. In multi-sheet workflows, users lose orientation, spend too much time scanning tabs, and struggle to maintain structure across related sheets.

## Target User

Power Excel users such as analysts, operators, and team members who work daily with medium-to-large workbooks containing many sheets and who need faster, clearer navigation without disrupting existing Excel habits.

## Core Pain Points

- The bottom tab bar does not scale well for large workbooks.
- Users waste time scanning horizontally across too many sheet tabs.
- Related sheets lack a modern organizational layer.
- Important sheets are difficult to keep constantly accessible.
- Hidden sheets exist in real workflows but are poorly surfaced.

## Product Vision

Build a modern worksheet navigation layer for Excel that complements the native tab bar instead of replacing it.

## High-Level Feature List

- Elegant workbook navigation panel
- Fast switching between worksheets
- Search / quick switcher
- Sheet groups with visual organization
- Pinning for important sheets
- Hidden sheets section
