Quick Installation

  1. 1
    Download the manifest.xml file.
  2. 2
    In Excel, go to Insert → Add-ins → Upload My Add-in.
  3. 3
    Select the downloaded manifest.xml and confirm.
  4. 4
    The Sheet Navigator button will appear in your Home tab.
