One add-in. Every Excel.

Same workflow across Web, Windows, and Mac. Pick your platform and install in minutes.

  1. 1
    Download excel-navbar-plugin.xml.
  2. 2
    Open office.com → Excel and create/open a workbook.
  3. 3
    Go to Home → Add-ins → More Settings.
  4. 4
    Select Upload My Add-in, choose excel-navbar-plugin.xml, and upload.
  1. 1

  2. 2
    Open Excel and create a blank workbook.
  3. 3
    Go to Home → Add-ins → My Add-ins.
  4. 4
